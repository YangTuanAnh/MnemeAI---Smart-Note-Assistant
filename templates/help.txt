Basic commands:
/start - <i>Start the bot and get a welcome message</i>
/help - <i>Get help and see available commands</i>

Add note and reminder commands:
/note - <i>Add a note</i>
/remind - <i>Set a reminder</i>

View and edit notes and reminders commands:
/view_notes - <i>View all notes and edit</i>
/view_reminders - <i>View all reminders and edit</i>

<b>Let me be your assistant! (Prompting)</b>
/ah - <i>Tell me to do</i>

Authorizing commands:
/notion_authorization - <i>Authorize Notion account</i>
/google_authorization - <i>Authorize Google account</i>

Connect to Notion:
/notion_page - <i>Connect to the user Notion page</i>

Cancel command:
/cancel - <i>Cancel the current operation</i>
