<b>🌟 Basic Commands 🌟</b><br>
- /start - <i>Start the bot and receive a warm welcome</i><br>
- /help - <i>Get assistance and explore available commands</i><br><br>

<b>✏️ Add Note and Reminder Commands ✏️</b><br>
- /note - <i>Add a quick note</i><br>
- /remind - <i>Schedule a helpful reminder</i><br><br>

<b>📚 View and Edit Notes and Reminders 📚</b><br>
- /view_notes - <i>Browse and manage your notes</i><br>
- /view_reminders - <i>Check and edit your reminders</i><br><br>

<b>🤖 Assistant at Your Service! 🤖</b><br>
- /ah - <i>Delegate tasks and let me assist you</i><br><br>

<b>🔑 Authorization Commands 🔑</b><br>
- /notion_authorization - <i>Authorize your Notion account</i><br>
- /google_authorization - <i>Connect with your Google account</i><br><br>

<b>🔗 Connect to Notion 🔗</b><br>
- /notion_page - <i>Link to your Notion page</i><br><br>

<b>⏰ Time and Timezone Commands ⏰</b><br>
- /show_time - <i>Display current time in your timezone</i><br>
- /timezone - <i>Set your preferred timezone</i><br><br>

<b>❌ Cancel Command ❌</b><br>
- /cancel - <i>Abort the ongoing operation</i><br>
