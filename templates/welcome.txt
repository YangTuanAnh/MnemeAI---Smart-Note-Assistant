Welcome to the <b>MnemeAI bot</b>! Enjoy fast note taking empowered by LLM. 

Here is what to do first!

<b> Set timezone </b>
- Use /timezone to set your timezone. This is an <b>important</b> step for the bot to work properly.

<b> Taking Notes </b>
1. Use /notion_authorization to connect your Notion account.
2. Choose a Notion page to save your notes.
3. Use /notion_page and paste the url of that Notion page.


<b> Add Reminders </b>
1. Use /google_authorization to connect your Google account.
2. Use /share_location to share your location with the bot. This is used to find the appropriate time zone.



Type /help to see available commands.
